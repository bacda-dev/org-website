BACDA · Brand guide 01 — Voice
Voice

The system, in one breath.

Kinetic Editorial / Concert-Hall Noir. Dark-first surfaces, warm amber accent pulled from the logo, performance photography as the anchor. Reads like a concert program — not a startup landing page.

Three principles

Dark first.

Public site renders on near-black ink. Photography sits on ink, the warm amber logo glows against it. Admin shell is the inverse — paper cream.

Editorial restraint.

Type does the heavy lifting — weight, tracking, hairlines, grain. Decoration is rare and used only at moments of emphasis.

Upright authority.

Display headings are upright Fraunces, weight 500, tight tracking. Italic is reserved for inline emphasis inside paragraph copy.

More than a dance group. A community built on bonding, love, and a passion for dance.

— BACDA, in its own words

Tone

Confident, not corporate. Warm, not soft. Editorial, not academic. We write like a printed playbill: short sentences, named productions, real years, no marketing-speak.

BACDA · Brand guide 03 — Typography
Typography · Three families

Fraunces, Inter Tight, JetBrains Mono.

A serif for display, a tight grotesk for body, a mono for editorial labels. Never Inter, Roboto, Poppins, Montserrat, or Arial.

Fraunces · Display

Characterful contemporary serif with strong italic voice. Used for every display heading on the site, always upright (weight 500), with tight tracking. Italic style is loaded but reserved for inline emphasis inside paragraph copy.

Don’ts

  • Don’t introduce a new color outside the palette. Derive states from existing tokens (opacity tints like text-cream/65, bg-amber/15).
  • Don’t use italic Fraunces on display headings. Reserved for inline emphasis only.
  • Don’t use system fonts for heading or body. Always font-display / font-sans / font-mono from the tokens.
  • Don’t add hard drop-shadows or glossy gradients. Surfaces stay matte; warmth comes from amber glow + grain.
  • Don’t ship inline style for static styling. Inline is acceptable only for a dynamic CSS custom property.
  • Don’t mix the brand acronym. The legal name is Bay Area Creative Dance Academy (B·A·C·D·A).
